Account Deletion

Delete in the app

The easiest way is directly in the app: go to Settings → Delete Account. Your account and all associated data will be removed immediately.

Request by email

You can also request deletion by emailing us. The request must come from the email address associated with your account so we can locate and remove your data. We'll process it within a reasonable timeframe.
